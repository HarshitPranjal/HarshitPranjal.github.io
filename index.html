About

I am an undergraduate in Mathematics & Computing at BIT Mesra, India, working on Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) — specifically Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) and neural operators for parameter estimation and inverse problems in fractional-order dynamical systems. My work also spans mathematical modeling of complex systems, including stability analysis and bifurcation theory for nonlinear dynamical systems.

I published in Chaos, Solitons & Fractals (Q1) in my second year of undergraduate study. I have authored and co-authored papers with faculty at BITS Pilani, VIT-AP University, and Covenant University, Nigeria. I am currently a research intern at IIT Delhi, investigating adversarial robustness and membership inference attacks on SciML models.

I am a recipient of the IAS-INSA-NASI Summer Research Fellowship 2026 (Mathematics) and the GP Birla Merit Scholarship.
